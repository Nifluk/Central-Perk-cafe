Arabica

The Arabian coffee tree is considered the most popular. Its fruits are characterized by a multifaceted taste and aroma. The culture is capricious and can be grown only in favorable climatic conditions

Liberica

The plant is called the Liberian coffee tree. The fruits grown on it are exported extremely rarely. As a rule, they are used in the country of origin. This is due to the whimsicality of culture.

Robusta

The fruits of the coffee tree are characterized by an increased concentration of caffeine. Due to this, the drink based on them has a bitterness. As a rule, robusta is included in the composition of mixtures to give them strength.
